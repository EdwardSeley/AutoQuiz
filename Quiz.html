Greetings! Here is Your Daily Latin Quiz:

  1. What does the Latin word coerceo mean?
    1. to put to flight, chase away, drive into exile, pursue.
    2. (adv) certainly, assuredly.
    3. to proclaim, announce, declaim.
    4. surround, enclose, restrain, confine.

  2. What does the Latin word placitum mean?
    1. allurements, charms, delights, fancies / sweetheart
    2. since, because.
    3. four times / again and again.
    4. accord, agreement, pact / assembly for judgment.

  3. What does the Latin word labes labis mean?
    1. hastening, hasty.
    2. excellent, distinguished, imminent.
    3. rejoicing, joy.
    4. stain, blemish, disgrace, infamy / misfortune.

  4. What does the Latin word cunabula mean?
    1. human being, man.
    2. cradle.
    3. it is agreed, it is resolved, it seems good.
    4. agreeable, pleasant, gratifying.

  5. What does the Latin word malum mean?
    1. rolling, revolving, turning around / changeable, inconstant.
    2. (+ dat.) to go to meet, oppose / help, remedy.
    3. to go forth, advance, proceed, go out.
    4. evil, misfortune, misdeed, crime, injury, damage.
