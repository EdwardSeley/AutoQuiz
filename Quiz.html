Greetings! Here is Your Daily Latin Quiz:

  1. What does the Latin word leto mean?
    1. book.
    2. to kill, slay.
    3. to groan, bellow, roar, low.
    4. various, varied.

  2. What does the Latin word creo mean?
    1. bad, wicked, injurious, culpable, evil.
    2. a little box, casket
    3. to create, make.
    4. resident, inhabitant of a place.

  3. What does the Latin word moveo moti motum mean?
    1. Alsace.
    2. faithlessly, disloyally.
    3. to move, arouse, affect, influence.
    4. touching, contact / contagion.

  4. What does the Latin word doleo mean?
    1. to carry out, bring forth.
    2. to suffer pain, to be pained, grieve.
    3. (neut. sing. abl.) (the eagerness) THROUGH WHICH he died.
    4. to set down, place / abandon, leave in the lurch.

  5. What does the Latin word eloquens mean?
    1. eloquent, persuasive, fluent.
    2. (adv.) before, previously, formerly.
    3. increase, enlargement, growth.
    4. madness, rage, frenzy.
